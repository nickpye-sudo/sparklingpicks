Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use glass cleaner on my TV or laptop screen?

No ‚Äî most glass cleaners contain ammonia or alcohol that can damage screen coatings. Use a dedicated screen cleaner or plain distilled water.

Why do my windows still look streaky?

Usually from using too much product, wiping with dirty cloths, or cleaning in sunlight. Less is more ‚Äî spray lightly and buff with clean microfibre.

Is vinegar as good as commercial glass cleaners?

Vinegar works for light cleaning but struggles with greasy fingerprints and doesn't evaporate as cleanly. Commercial cleaners are formulated to leave no residue.

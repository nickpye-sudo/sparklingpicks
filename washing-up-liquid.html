Frequently Asked Questions

Is expensive washing up liquid worth it?

Often, yes. Premium products like Fairy are more concentrated, so you use less per wash. Calculate the cost per wash, not the bottle price. A ¬£3 bottle that lasts 100 washes is cheaper than a ¬£1 bottle that lasts 30.

Are eco-friendly options as effective?

For everyday dishes, yes. Products like Ecover handle normal grease well. For heavy-duty jobs (burnt pans, roasting trays), conventional products still have an edge.

Can washing up liquid irritate skin?

Yes, especially fragranced products. If you have sensitive skin, try Ecover Zero or wear rubber gloves. Rinse hands thoroughly after washing up.

The Bottom Line

For most households, Fairy Original offers the best balance of cleaning power and value. Buy in bulk for the best price. If you prefer eco-friendly products, Ecover is effective for daily use. Those with sensitive skin should try Ecover Zero.

On a tight budget? Astonish or supermarket own-brands handle everyday dishes well, just don't expect miracles on tough grease.

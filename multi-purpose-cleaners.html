Frequently Asked Questions

Can multi-purpose cleaner replace all my other products?

For about 80% of household cleaning, yes. You'll still want specialists for: heavy limescale, oven interiors, toilet bowls, mould, and heavy kitchen grease. But for daily tidying, one good multi-surface spray covers most jobs.

Is antibacterial really necessary?

For most cleaning, no ‚Äî physically removing dirt with any cleaner is usually enough. Antibacterial is worth it for: after handling raw meat, during illness, on high-touch surfaces with vulnerable people in the house, and childcare settings.

Can I use multi-purpose on wood furniture?

Generally yes for sealed/varnished wood. Avoid on untreated wood, antiques, or waxed surfaces ‚Äî use dedicated wood polish instead. Always test on a hidden area first.

What about mixing cleaners?

Never mix cleaning products ‚Äî especially bleach with anything. Even mixing multi-purpose cleaners can create harmful fumes. Use one product, rinse, then switch if needed.

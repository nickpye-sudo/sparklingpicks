Frequently Asked Questions

Can drain cleaner damage pipes?

With occasional use, modern drain cleaners are safe for most pipes. However, frequent use of caustic cleaners can damage older pipes, especially PVC. Enzyme-based cleaners are safest for regular maintenance.

Why didn't the drain cleaner work?

Chemical cleaners dissolve organic matter (hair, grease, soap). They won't work on: solid objects (toys, caps), mineral buildup, or blockages deep in the main sewer line. For these, you need a plumber.

Is it safe to use drain cleaner in a garbage disposal?

Most are safe, but check the label. Never pour caustic soda into a disposal â€” it can damage the mechanism. For disposals, enzyme cleaners or specific disposal cleaning products are safer.

How often should I clean my drains?

Monthly enzyme treatment prevents most blockages. Don't use caustic cleaners preventatively â€” they're for existing blockages only. Regular hot water flushes help too.

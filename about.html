Our Mission

Sparkling Picks exists to help you make informed decisions about household cleaning products. We believe that choosing the right cleaner shouldn't require hours of research or trial and error with products that don't work.

What We Do

We research and compare cleaning products available in the UK, focusing on:

  • Effectiveness — Does it actually clean?
  • Value — Is it worth the money?
  • Safety — Is it suitable for your needs (sensitive skin, pets, etc.)?
  • Environmental impact — What are the eco credentials?

Our Values

  • Honesty — We tell you the downsides, not just the positives
  • Independence — We don't accept payment for reviews
  • Practicality — Real-world advice for real homes
  • Transparency — Clear about how we make money

How We Make Money

Sparkling Picks is a participant in the Amazon Associates Programme. When you click our links and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. This helps us keep the site running and continue providing free content.

Importantly: affiliate relationships never influence our recommendations. We recommend products we genuinely believe are good choices, whether or not they offer affiliate commissions.

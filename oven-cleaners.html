Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I clean my oven?

A quick wipe after messy cooking prevents buildup. Deep clean every 3-6 months depending on use. Self-cleaning ovens still benefit from occasional manual cleaning.

Can oven cleaners damage my oven?

Caustic cleaners can damage rubber seals, painted surfaces, and aluminium. Avoid contact with heating elements. Always check manufacturer guidance for your specific oven.

What about the self-clean function?

Self-cleaning cycles work by heating to extremely high temperatures, burning off grease. Effective but uses lots of energy and creates smoke. Best combined with occasional manual cleaning.

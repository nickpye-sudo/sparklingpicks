Frequently Asked Questions

Do I actually need fabric softener?

Technically, no. It's not essential for cleaning. But it does make clothes feel softer, reduces static, makes ironing easier, and leaves a pleasant scent. Whether that's worth the money is personal preference.

Why shouldn't I use softener on towels?

Fabric softener coats fibres with a thin waxy layer ‚Äî that's how it makes things soft. On towels, this coating reduces absorbency over time. Use white vinegar instead, or just skip softener for towel loads.

Can fabric softener cause skin irritation?

Yes, especially fragranced ones. The chemicals and perfumes stay on clothes and contact your skin all day. If you notice itching or rashes, switch to a fragrance-free option like Surcare.

Liquid softener vs dryer sheets?

Liquid goes in the washing machine and coats fibres during the rinse cycle. Dryer sheets work during tumble drying. Liquid is generally better value and more effective; sheets are convenient for freshening up already-clean items.

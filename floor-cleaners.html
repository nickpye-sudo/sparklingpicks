üè† Quick Floor Type Guide

  • Tile/Porcelain: Almost anything works. Flash, Zoflora, or budget options all fine.
  • Laminate: Use damp (not wet) mop. Avoid excessive water. Flash or Method work well.
  • Hardwood: Use Bona or specialist wood cleaner only. Too much water damages wood.
  • Vinyl/LVT: Flash, Method, or any pH-neutral cleaner. Avoid abrasives.
  • Stone (marble/slate): pH-neutral cleaners only. No vinegar or acidic products.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why do my floors feel sticky after mopping?

Usually from using too much product or not rinsing. Dilute cleaners properly and consider a final pass with plain water.

Can I use the same cleaner in every room?

If all your floors are the same type, yes. Flash works on tile, laminate, and vinyl. Only hardwood needs a specialist cleaner.

Is Zoflora safe around pets?

Yes, once fully dry. Zoflora is toxic to cats and dogs while wet, so ensure the floor is completely dry before allowing pet access.

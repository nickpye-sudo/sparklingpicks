How we test carpet cleaners

Our testing process

Stain application

We apply standardised amounts of red wine, coffee, mud, and (for pet-specific products) synthetic urine to identical carpet samples. Stains are left to dry for 24 hours before testing â€” simulating real-world conditions where you don't notice a spill immediately.

Cleaning test

Each product is applied according to manufacturer instructions. We allow the recommended contact time, then blot or vacuum as directed. The carpet is left to dry completely before assessment.

Scoring

A panel assesses stain removal on a scale. We also check for residue, colour fastness (whether the cleaner affected carpet colour), and ease of use. Products are tested on both light and dark carpet samples.

Note: We buy all products at full retail price. Manufacturers have no input into our testing or results.

How we test mould removers

Our testing process

Test surfaces

We test on three surfaces: ceramic tiles (typical bathroom walls), grout (where mould often concentrates), and silicone sealant (the most challenging surface). All test panels have established mould growth of at least 4 weeks.

Removal test

Each product is applied according to manufacturer instructions. We assess visible mould removal at 15, 30, and 60 minutes. Products are scored on how completely they remove mould without scrubbing.

Regrowth prevention

The treated panels are placed in a warm, humid environment for 4 weeks. We then assess how much mould has returned. Products that only bleach (whiten) the mould without killing it score poorly here.

Note: We buy all products at full retail price. Manufacturers have no input into our testing or results.

How to prevent mould

Removing mould is half the battle. To prevent it coming back:

  • Ventilate: Open windows or run the extractor fan during and after showers
  • Wipe down: Squeegee shower walls after use to remove moisture
  • Fix leaks: Damp from hidden leaks causes persistent mould
  • Check sealant: If silicone sealant is badly affected, replace it entirely
  • Regular treatment: Spray mould-prone areas weekly with a preventative product

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I still need rinse aid with all-in-one tablets?

In hard water areas, you might benefit from additional rinse aid for spotless glassware. In soft water areas, all-in-one tablets usually suffice.

Should I use half a tablet for small loads?

Modern tablets are designed as single-dose. Cutting them in half can reduce effectiveness and may not dissolve properly. Run a full load instead to save money.

Why do my glasses come out cloudy?

Usually caused by hard water limescale or using too much detergent. Try adding rinse aid, check your salt levels, and ensure you're using quality tablets.

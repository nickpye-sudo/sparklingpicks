How we test bathroom cleaners

Our testing process

Soap scum test

We apply soap and body wash to ceramic tiles and leave them to dry for 24 hours â€” simulating a shower that hasn't been cleaned for a week. We then spray the cleaner, leave for the recommended time, and wipe with a cloth. We assess how much residue remains.

Limescale test

Using standardised limescale deposits on chrome-effect tiles (typical of bathroom taps and fixtures), we apply each cleaner for 5 minutes. We measure how much limescale is dissolved and how easily the remainder wipes away.

Scoring

Each product is scored on limescale removal (40%), soap scum removal (40%), and ease of use (20%). Ease of use includes spray mechanism quality, instructions clarity, and whether the product requires scrubbing.

Note: We buy all products at full retail price. Manufacturers have no input into our testing or results.

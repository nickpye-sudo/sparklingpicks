Frequently Asked Questions

How do I remove black mould from bathroom sealant?

Spray with a mould remover containing bleach (like HG Mould Spray). Leave for 30 minutes, then wipe. For stubborn mould, the sealant may need replacing.

Can I mix bathroom cleaners?

Never mix bleach with ammonia or acidic cleaners ‚Äî this creates toxic gases. Use one product at a time and rinse between products.

How often should I deep clean the bathroom?

Weekly wipe-downs prevent buildup. A deep clean (grout, behind toilet, shower seals) every 2-4 weeks is usually sufficient.
